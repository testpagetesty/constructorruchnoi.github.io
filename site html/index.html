Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our mobile game development platform.

What platforms do you support?

We support Android and iOS mobile platforms with tools optimized for native and hybrid development.

Can I use the platform without coding experience?

Yes. Our tools are designed for both coders and non-coders with visual scripting and drag-and-drop interfaces.

Is there a free plan available?

Yes. We offer a free tier with access to core features, ideal for learning and small prototype projects.

Do you allow monetization features?

Yes, but only ethical monetization options such as ads, in-app purchases, and subscriptions. No gambling or loot-box mechanics are allowed.

Where is your team based?

Our core team is based in India, providing local support and understanding of the Indian development ecosystem.

How can I submit my game for publishing?

You can apply through your dashboard. Our team will review your submission and assist with compliance and publishing steps.

Are there community resources or forums?

Yes. We have an active developer community, including forums, Discord groups, and monthly webinars for learning and collaboration.
